# LeafletPilot Brand Notes

## Final Project Name

**LeafletPilot**

## One-Line Description

LeafletPilot turns product lists into weekly market leaflets, brochures, PDFs and social media visuals in minutes.

## Türkçe Açıklama

LeafletPilot, marketlerin ve yerel gıda işletmelerinin kampanya ürün listelerini mesajlaşma uygulaması üzerinden alarak otomatik broşür, PDF ve sosyal medya görselleri oluşturan AI destekli bir SaaS platformudur.

## Product Positioning

Primary positioning:

> LeafletPilot is a messaging-first brochure automation platform for local markets, halal stores, butchers and grocery retailers.

Turkish positioning:

> LeafletPilot, yerel marketler, helal marketler, kasaplar ve şarküteriler için mesajlaşma uygulaması üzerinden çalışan kampanya broşürü otomasyon platformudur.

## Naming Rationale

LeafletPilot is stronger than generic names because it combines:

- A clear output: leaflet / brochure
- A control metaphor: pilot
- SaaS-friendly tone
- Future expansion beyond only grocery markets

It can later support:

- Restaurant menu campaigns
- Butcher/deli flyers
- Greengrocer weekly offers
- Wholesale catalogs
- Social media promo visuals
- Printed campaign PDFs
